How Work Orders are Generated

Work orders will be created for each production stage with the specified quantities:

  • Gurat (Cutting): Initial cutting and preparation of materials
  • Assembly: Putting together the shoe components
  • Press: Pressing and molding operations
  • Finishing: Final touches, polishing, and quality checks
Note: Only stages with quantities greater than 0 will have work orders created. The work orders will be scheduled sequentially based on the start date and duration specified.
